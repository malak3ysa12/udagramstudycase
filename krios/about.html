About Krios

Small Business Software at Small Business Prices

Krios was created with the vision of offering small business the chance to design and create their own software at prices they can afford. We have realized that the stigma for overpriced software has taken over the working world, and we aim to change that. With software that can organize and manage your data, you can explore the true potential of your small Businsess. And we will be right there with you.

When you work with Krios software development, we will work as your IT department. You can rely on us to create you an effective and durrable application, which your company can use to increase productivity and make your business process more effective. "Gone are the days of paper based business" is a term we already know, "Gone are the days of chunky overpriced software" is a term you will learn with Krios.

How We work:

  1. You and a member of the Krios Account managment team will meet, and define the requirements for your software application.
  2. Krios will provide you with a flexible quote based on the complexity of the software you will need.
  3. After confirmation, Krios will design you your software within the timeline agreed upon, and launch your new Business Application for you and your Employees to use.

Its honestly just as simple as that. Its time to take the next step with your small Business.
